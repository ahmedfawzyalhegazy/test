Chairman’s Message

Welcome to 1st Kuwait Pediatric Association Conference and Workshop

Distinguished Guests,

With great pleasure and honor as the chairman of the 1st Kuwait Pediatric Association Conference and Workshop i welcome you all. It is a momentous occasion for us to gather and discuss the critical subject of pediatric emergency care. I would like to extend my warmest welcome to all the participants, speakers, and attendees who have joined us from various corners of the world.

Our mission with this conference is to enhance the knowledge and skills of healthcare professionals in the field of pediatric emergency care. The well-being and health of our children are of paramount importance, and it is our responsibility as medical practitioners to be well-prepared and equipped to handle any emergency situation that may arise.

During three days of the event, we have an exceptional lineup of speakers who are experts in their respective fields. They will be sharing their invaluable insights and experiences, providing us with the latest advancements and best practices in pediatric emergency care. From discussions on trauma management to resuscitation techniques, from infectious diseases to critical care interventions, we have a comprehensive program that covers a wide range of essential topics.

But this conference is not just about lectures and presentations; it is also about fostering collaboration and sharing knowledge. We have organized interactive workshops that will allow participants to engage in hands-on learning experiences. These workshops will provide an opportunity to practice skills, exchange ideas, and build networks with colleagues from around the world.

I would like to express my deepest gratitude to the organizing committee, who worked tirelessly to bring this event to fruition. Their dedication and commitment are truly commendable. I would also like to thank our sponsors and exhibitors for their generous support, as without them, this conference would not have been possible.

I urge all participants to actively engage in the sessions, ask questions, and take full advantage of the expertise present here. Let us use this platform to expand our knowledge, refine our skills, and ultimately improve the quality of care we provide to our young patients.

Finally, I would like to reiterate the importance of collaboration and ongoing education. Pediatric emergency care is a constantly evolving field, and it is crucial for us to stay updated with the latest research and advancements. Together, we can make a significant impact on the lives of children, ensuring that they receive the best possible care when faced with emergencies.

Once again, I extend my warmest welcome to all of you. May this conference be a fruitful and enlightening experience for everyone involved.

Thank you
Yours Sincerely,

Dr. Abdullah Shamsah

Consultant Pediatric and Consultant of Pediatric Emergency Medicine.
Chairman of Kuwait Pediatric Association
